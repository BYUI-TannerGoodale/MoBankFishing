Welcome to Missouri River Bank Fishing

MoRiverBankFishing.com is a blog/affiliate website dedicated to educating the public about the mighty river systems of Missouri and some of the different ways to fish them from the bank. We share our journeys and experiences, as well as rigs and bait choices along the way. The goal is to have fun, be safe and motivate you to get out on the water, no matter where you're at.

If you're interested in starting your own fishing adventure, check out our gear page for links to products and gear we recommend for new and experienced fisher-folk alike.
